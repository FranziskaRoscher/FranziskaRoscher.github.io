Data




Over the years, I have collected various documents from a range of government and other official website, not all of which are accessible from outside India. This page serves as a repository for these data, which form the base of much of my research.


Electoral Data

The files below are official election reports, downloaded from the website of the Election Commission of India (ECI).


Lok Sabha Election Reports

Before 2009, Volume I of the Lok Sabha election report contains national and state-level summaries, while Volume II contains detailed results. Starting in 2009, the format of Lok Sabha election reports changed, and data has been published in smaller files.
